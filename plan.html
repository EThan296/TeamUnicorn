Group Assignment

Group Name: Team Unicorn

Mission Statement

Our mission involves recruiting and including new members/volunteers, increasing ticket sales and attendance, and attracting more performers that want to promote themselves.

The primary goal of the website is to increase awareness of local talent through promotion of the TCMC-organised concerts.

Success Evaluation

An increase in members and sold tickets will point to a successfull web page. To monitor whether members signed up because of the page, this should be a question in the sign-up form. More involvement from current members as well as from the new members, as volunteers or performers, indicates that the site has suceeded in the goals, assuming this is the only change in advertising from TCMC.

We can tell if we have succeded with our mission statement if we have:

  • Improved ticket sales
  • More volunteers helping out
  • More concerts
  • Have more performers signing up and wanting to perform

Target Audience

The target audience is mainly people over 40 years old, mostly with a specific taste in music, although with great diversity (depends on the theme of the concert).

To accommodate for this age group, the web page should offer accessibility for people with visual impairment. This means appropriate fonts, colour schemes and layout using a traditional, intuitive navigation that is easy to use, even for people with varying computer skills.

Content

  • Home page - Provides easy links to popular content within the website.
  • Events - Lists all available upcoming events with relevant information displayed.
  • Artists - Displays all registered artists and groups with relevant information in dynamic drop down tabs for easy access.
  • Forum/ Bulletin - A page for discussion and general updates for the website.
  • Join us/Register - a page for people to provide details and sign up/opt in to the website and email newsletter.
  • About - Displays information and brief history about the TCMC
  • Contact - Displays all contact information for the TCMC
